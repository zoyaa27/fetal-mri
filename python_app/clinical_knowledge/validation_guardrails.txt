Rule 1: Ventriculomegaly Threshold
- Normal fetal lateral ventricles must measure LESS than 10.0 mm.
- Mild Ventriculomegaly: 10.0 mm to 12.0 mm.
- Moderate Ventriculomegaly: 12.1 mm to 15.0 mm.
- Severe Ventriculomegaly: Greater than 15.0 mm.
- GUARDRAIL: If any measurement exceeds 10.0 mm, the report IMPRESSION must explicitly include the word "Ventriculomegaly" and recommend a follow-up ultrasound or MRI in 2–4 weeks.

Rule 2: De-identification Verification
- The pipeline must reject files containing raw patient names or clear text dates of birth before compiling a report.
